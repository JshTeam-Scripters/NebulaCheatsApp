What is Sideloading?

Sideloading allows you to install applications or profiles on your device outside official app stores.

  • It grants you freedom beyond app store restrictions.
  • Can be risky if installing untrusted software.
  • Common in iOS and Android customization communities.
  • Requires special configuration profiles or APKs.

Always verify the source of sideloaded content to protect your device.
